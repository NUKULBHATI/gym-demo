About PowerHouse

Our Story

Our Story

Building a Community of Champions

Founded in 2012, PowerHouse Fitness began with a simple mission: to create a gym that truly cares about its members. What started as a small facility with basic equipment has grown into one of the most respected fitness centers in the region.

We believe that fitness is not just about physical strength, but about building mental resilience, fostering community, and creating lasting lifestyle changes. Our approach combines cutting-edge science with genuine human connection.

Our Mission

To empower every individual to achieve their fullest potential through world-class fitness facilities, expert guidance, and an unwavering commitment to their success. We strive to make fitness accessible, enjoyable, and transformative for people from all walks of life.

Our Vision

To become the leading fitness destination that inspires a global movement toward healthier, stronger, and more confident communities. We envision a world where everyone has the tools and support to live their best life through fitness.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

What are your operating hours?

We are open 24/7 for members with keycard access. Staffed hours are Monday through Friday from 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM, and Saturday through Sunday from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Holiday hours may vary.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes! We offer a complimentary 7-day trial that includes full access to all gym facilities, group classes, and one personal training session. No credit card required to start your trial.

Can I freeze my membership?

Absolutely. Members can freeze their membership for up to 3 months per year for medical reasons, travel, or personal circumstances. A small administrative fee may apply depending on your membership plan.

What is your cancellation policy?

Monthly memberships can be cancelled with 30 days notice. Annual memberships can be cancelled with 60 days notice. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new members who are not satisfied.

Do you have locker rooms and showers?

Yes, we have premium locker rooms with secure digital lockers, spacious showers with premium toiletries, saunas, and steam rooms. Towel service is included with all membership plans.

Are personal trainers included in the membership?

Personal training sessions are included based on your membership tier. Monthly members get 2 sessions per month, while Annual members receive 4 sessions per month. Additional sessions can be purchased at member rates.
